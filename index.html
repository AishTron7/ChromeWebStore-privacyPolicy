Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your personal data when you use our Chrome Web Store extensions developed by Aishwary Shukla.

Data Collection

Our Chrome Web Store extension does not collect any personal user data. However, when you use our review summary extension on Flipkart.com, we may collect reviews of the product you are viewing in order to provide you with a summary.

Data Use

We use the reviews collected through our review summary extension solely to provide you with a summary of the product reviews. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personal user data collected through our Chrome Web Store extension with third parties. However, we may share the reviews collected through our review summary extension with our API server provider in order to generate a summary.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of data collected through our review summary extension, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect this data.

Data Retention

We will retain the reviews collected through our review summary extension only for as long as necessary to provide you with a summary of the product reviews. We will then delete this data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data handling practices. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
